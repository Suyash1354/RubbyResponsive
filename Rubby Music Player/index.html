About Us

Hello, I’m Siddesh. I created an app called “Rubby Music Player”. The story behind this app is quite interesting.
My friend, Suyash, was in love with a girl named Rubby. He had been in love with her for a year but hadn’t told her.
After a year, he decided to propose to her. He asked a female friend to find out if Rubby was single, but unfortunately,
she was already committed. Suyash was quite upset, but he didn’t let it bother him too much.
As his good friend, I suggested a song to him called ‘Tu Pyar Hai Kisi Aur Ka Tuzhe Chahata Koi Aur Hai’.
He loved that song. Then, I had a crazy idea to make a music player app.
I was wondering what I should name the app when I suddenly realized I should name it “Rubby”.
Suyash also liked the idea, so I went ahead and made the app. One more thing, the logo of the app and this website is made by Suyash.
It’s actually a real depiction of Rubby.
Thanks for Reading to our story.
